To generate the figures presented in the Weise et al (2026) article, the user will need to populate this folder with required geographical data from the following sources:

https://www.donneesquebec.ca/recherche/dataset/grhq (rivers and lakes in Quebec)
https://www.gnb.ca/en/campaign/geonb/data-catalogue/hydrographic-network.html (rivers and lakes in New Brunswick)
https://www.hydrosheds.org/products/hydrolakes (an alternative source of lake data, used for some figures)https://www.hydrosheds.org/products/hydrobasins (watershed outlines)

Consult individual scripts for specific requirements
